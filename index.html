Make Networking Efficient and Painless

Assassin is a professional networking app that allows users to see basic professional details about the people near them at a networking event. The app uses Core Location and GeoFire to update a user's location and query users nearby in real time.

Built in Three Weeks

Assassin was built in under three weeks by students at DevMountain - a iOS development school in Salt Lake City. The concept, UX, UI, code, and testing was all done during the three week period using Swift.
